The Best Movies According to Sarah

❤️ My top 4 movies of all time ❤️

1. Lord of the Rings

This is my most favorite movie. I love the book and I absolutely love the movie.

2. North and South

Adaptation from the book of the same title by Elizabeth Gaskell.

3. Sabrina (1995)

A remake of the 1954 movie starring Harrison Ford and Julia Ormond.

4. While You Were Sleeping

A sweet rom-com starring Sandra Bullock and Bill Pullman.
